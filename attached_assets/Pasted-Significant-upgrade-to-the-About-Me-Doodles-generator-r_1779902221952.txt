Significant upgrade to the About Me / Doodles generator: replace the existing word + arrow approach with 3D puffy sticker labels.

NEW STICKER DESIGN PER WORD:
1. A rounded rectangle panel (border-radius around 20px) with white background
2. The word in bold uppercase, dark text colour (default near-black)
3. A chunky 2-layer outline border in the user's chosen accent colour (outer layer slightly darker shade, inner layer the chosen colour, creates the puffy 3D effect)
4. A soft drop shadow underneath (8px y-offset, 16px blur, 30% opacity black)
5. Slight rotation per sticker (-8° to +8°, randomised) for casual placement feel
6. Padding inside the sticker: 18px horizontal, 12px vertical

DECORATIVE TOPPERS:
Each sticker has ONE topper element peeking out of the top edge:
- Rainbow arch (3-band, pink/blue/yellow, sized roughly 60% of sticker height)
- Puffy 3D heart (pink, slight gradient for dimension)
- Chunky 3D star (5-point, white fill with accent-colour outline)

User can:
- Pick the default topper for all stickers via a dropdown ("Rainbows" / "Hearts" / "Stars" / "Mixed")
- Override the topper per individual sticker if they want a mix
- Optional small sparkle SVG in opposite corner for extra flair

INTERACTION:
- Stickers remain fully draggable on the preview canvas (existing behaviour)
- Resizable via corner handles
- Rotatable via top handle
- Free-position the same as before
- The accent colour picker now controls the sticker outline AND topper outline colour (not the panel background, which stays white)

REPLACES:
The previous "text + arrow" word elements. Arrows are no longer used (the sticker IS the visual element, more memorable than an arrow line).

The cut-out subject photo, glow, blurred background and title still work as before. Only the WORD elements are getting upgraded to stickers.
